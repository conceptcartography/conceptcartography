# distributive epistemic injustice

## 📖 Definitions

> "injustice in the distribution of the epistemic good of knowledge" (Coady 2010, 112)

## 🔗 Relations

- **type of**: [epistemic injustice](./epistemic-injustice.md)

## 📚 References
